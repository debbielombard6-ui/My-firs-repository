Introduction to yoga

yoga is a mind-body practice that combines movement,breathing and mindfullness to improve physical health and mental well-being.

Vinyasa Yoga

Vinyasa yoga focuses on flowing movements that are syncronized with breath,it is dynamic and helps build strength and flexibillity.

Hata Yoga

Hata yoga is slower-paced practice that emphasizes basic postures and breathing techniques,making it ideal for beginners.

Kundalini yoga

Kundalini yoga combines movement,breathwork,and meditation to promote deep relaxation and heighten awareness.
